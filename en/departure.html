👋 Check-out and departure

Thank you for choosing Gravassryggen. Before you leave, please go through the checklist below.

🕛 Check-out

Check-out is no later than 11:00.

Late check-out may be possible for an additional fee if the cabin is available. Please contact us if this is desired.

✅ Checklist before departure

  • Tidy up after yourselves and leave the cabin in a neat condition.
  • Empty the fridge and freezer of food you brought with you.
  • Place used dishes in the dishwasher and start it.
  • Take all waste with you and deliver it to one of the local waste stations.
  • Let us know if anything is broken, missing or not working as it should.
  • Check that all windows are closed.
  • Turn off all lights and make sure any candles are extinguished.
  • Lock the terrace door and both entrance doors.

🔑 Key

Return the key to the pick-up location before you leave.

💬 We appreciate feedback

If you have suggestions for improvements or experienced something we should know about, we would greatly appreciate hearing from you.

Feedback helps us make the experience even better for future guests.

❤️ Thank you for visiting

We hope you have had a wonderful stay at Gravassryggen and return home with good memories from the mountains.

Thank you for taking good care of the cabin. You are warmly welcome back!

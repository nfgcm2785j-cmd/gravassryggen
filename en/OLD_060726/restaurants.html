🍽 Food, restaurants and shops

Here are our recommendations for groceries, restaurants and shopping in the area.

← Back to front page

🛒 Joker Sinnes

Joker Sinnes is only a few minutes’ drive from the cabin and is the nearest grocery store.

The store has a good selection of groceries, fresh bread, fruit and vegetables, fuel, EV charging and a limited selection of pharmacy products.

🍽 Slottet i Sirdal

Slottet i Sirdal is a popular place to eat in Sirdal, with good food and fantastic views.

Here you can enjoy lunch, dinner or something nice to drink after a day outdoors.

🍔 Sirdal Lodge

Sirdal Lodge serves food with views towards the mountains, and is a pleasant place to end an active day.

🏘 Tonstad centre

Tonstad is about 30 minutes from the cabin and has a wider range of shops and services.

Here you will find grocery stores, a pharmacy, café, emergency medical service and other facilities.

🕯 Byrkjedalstunet

Byrkjedalstunet is a popular stop between Sirdal and Stavanger.

Here you will find a restaurant, bakery, candle factory and an interesting shop with local products and interior items. It is also a favourite with children.

A lovely place to stop either on the way to or from the cabin.

🛍 Ålgård

If you would like more shopping options, Ålgård is located along the E39 towards Stavanger.

Here you will find Norwegian Outlet, Vinmonopolet, several well-known shop chains, restaurants and a shopping centre.

If you are looking for a particular place to eat or would like local tips, just ask us. We are happy to share our favourites.

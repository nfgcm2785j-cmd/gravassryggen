⛰ Activities

Sirdal offers wonderful nature experiences all year round. Here are some of our recommendations in the area.

🥾 Explore hiking options with the UT app

We recommend the free UT app from the Norwegian Trekking Association (DNT) if you would like hiking suggestions in the area.

The app shows marked trails, detailed maps and hikes at all difficulty levels. Maps can also be downloaded for offline use.

Read more about UT →

🌲 Høyt & Lavt Sirdal

Just a short drive from the cabin is Høyt & Lavt Sirdal – one of the region’s most popular family activities.

Here you will find climbing courses, ziplines and challenges for both children and adults.

🏖 The beach by Høyt & Lavt

Right by the climbing park there is a lovely bathing area with a sandy beach and swimming jetty.

Perfect for a relaxing summer day after a mountain walk.

⛳ Sirdal Fjellgolf

Sirdal Fjellgolf offers a great golf experience in beautiful mountain surroundings.

The course is suitable for both experienced golfers and beginners.

🎣 Fishing

The area has many good fishing lakes with beautiful mountain trout.

See the separate information page about fishing permits and fishing areas around Gravassryggen.

🚵 Cycling

Sirdal has many nice gravel roads and trails that are well suited for cycling, both with regular bikes and e-bikes.

🦌 Experience nature

Take a quiet walk in the area and enjoy the beautiful nature. If you are lucky, you may spot moose, fox, hare, black grouse or golden eagle.

🎿 Winter activities

In winter, Sirdal is known for great alpine resorts, prepared cross-country ski trails and fantastic winter conditions.

There is easy access to alpine skiing, cross-country skiing and winter activities for the whole family.

🍽 Food and groceries

At Sinnes you will find Joker, restaurants, cafés, fuel and EV charging.

It is only a few minutes’ drive from the cabin.

❤️ Our best tips

Please ask us if you would like recommendations for hikes, fishing lakes or other activities. We are happy to share our local favourites.

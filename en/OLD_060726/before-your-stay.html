🎒 Before your stay

Practical information that is useful before arriving at Gravassryggen.

← Back to front page

📍 Address

Gravassryggen 18
4443 Tjørhom, Sirdal

Apple Maps and Google Maps will take you directly to the cabin.

🕒 Check-in

Check-in is from 16:00.

Early check-in may be possible for an additional fee if the cabin is ready.

🔑 Key

Instructions are sent in a separate Airbnb message the day before check-in.

Please remember to lock the cabin when you leave it.

🏡 Private use of the cabin

Your stay includes private use of the entire cabin and property.

The owner does not live on the property and will not stop by during your stay unless agreed, requested by guests or necessary in an emergency.

🚗 Parking

There is parking directly by the cabin.

Additional parking is available at the shared parking area by the road, about a 3-minute walk from the cabin.

📶 WiFi and mobile coverage

The cabin has fast Starlink internet.

The WiFi network name and password are sent in a separate Airbnb message.

There is also 4G/5G coverage in the area.

🛏 Bed linen and towels

Bed linen and towels are not included and must be brought by guests.

If you forget them, please contact us in good time. It may be possible to arrange rental of bed linen and towels for an additional fee.

Bed sizes

  • 1 bed – 180 × 200 cm
  • 1 bed – 150 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 120 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 80 × 200 cm

Pillow size: 50 × 70 cm
Duvet size: 140 × 200 cm

🛒 Grocery store

The nearest grocery store is Joker Sinnes, about 5 minutes by car from the cabin.

The store offers groceries, fruit and vegetables, fresh bread, household items, fuel, EV charging and a small selection of pharmacy products.

⚡ Electric fence

An electric sheep fence surrounds parts of the property.

Please close all gates after passing through so the sheep do not enter the plot.

The fence has low voltage and is not dangerous, but small children should be supervised.

💧 Drinking water

The cabin has fresh well water from the mountains.

The water is safe to drink and is of very good quality.

📞 Contact

If you have questions before or during your stay, please get in touch. Airbnb messages are preferred unless it is urgent.

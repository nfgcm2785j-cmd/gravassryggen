Practical information and useful advice for a safe and pleasant winter stay.

🚗 Parking and snow clearing

It is possible to park by the cabin.

Because of heavy snow, it is normally not possible to turn the car around by the cabin in winter. You should therefore expect to reverse.

Alternatively, the car can be parked at the shared parking area by the road, about a 3-minute walk from the cabin.

The road is normally cleared in the morning and afternoon. If heavy snowfall is forecast overnight and you need to leave early, it may be wise to park at the shared parking area.

🔥 Using the fireplace

The cabin is heated with electric panel heaters, underfloor heating and a fireplace. The electric heating normally provides sufficient warmth, so the fireplace can be used in winter if you would like extra heat or a cosy atmosphere.

Any firewood in the basket by the fireplace is included and may be used. If you need more firewood, it can normally be purchased at Joker.

The firewood stored outside by the cabin is wet and must not be used.

Lighting the fire

  • Use plenty of kindling and open the air vent fully.
  • Leave the stove door slightly ajar in the resting position until the fire is burning well and the draft is stable.
  • Then close the stove door fully and let the fire burn.
  • Only add larger logs once a good bed of embers has formed.
  • If the new logs do not catch fire right away, the stove door can again be left slightly ajar in the resting position until the wood has caught fire. This may take a little time.
  • Let the flames establish properly before closing the stove door fully again.

⚠️ Important

Using the kitchen extractor fan while the fireplace is in use can cause smoke to be drawn into the living room.

If the extractor fan must be used while the fire is burning, we recommend opening a window slightly.

🚙 Winter driving

Roads in Sirdal can be snowy and slippery during winter.

We recommend good winter tyres. In heavy snowfall or difficult conditions, four-wheel drive or snow chains can be an advantage.

🥾 Moving around in winter

Please be aware that the terrace, stairs and outdoor areas can be slippery.

Ice grips can be a good investment if you plan to walk a lot.

🌨 Weather and clothing

Mountain weather can change quickly.

We recommend windproof outerwear, warm shoes, a hat and gloves if you will be outside for a longer period.

It is a good idea to check the weather forecast before heading out.

🎿 Winter activities

Sirdal offers great alpine resorts, prepared cross-country ski trails and many lovely winter walks.

See the Activities page for tips about things to do in the area.

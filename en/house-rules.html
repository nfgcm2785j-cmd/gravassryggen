🏡 Take care of the cabin

Gravassryggen is our family cabin. We really appreciate you treating the cabin, its contents and the outdoor area with the same care as if it were your own.

👟 Shoes indoors

Please take off outdoor shoes at the entrance. Feel free to use slippers or indoor shoes inside the cabin.

🚭 Smoking

Smoking is not allowed indoors.

If you smoke outside, please make sure cigarette ends are fully extinguished and disposed of properly.

🐕 Pets

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed.

🎉 Parties and events

The cabin is intended for quiet holiday stays.

Parties, events and larger gatherings are not allowed.

🌙 Please respect the neighbours

The cabin is located in a quiet cabin area.

Please keep noise to a moderate level, especially between 23:00 and 07:00.

🔥 Candles and fire safety

Never leave candles unattended.

Always extinguish candles before leaving the cabin or going to bed.

Please use fire and heat with common sense, and contact us if anything seems unusual.

⚡ Electric vehicle charging

Electric vehicles must only be charged from an approved charging point. Use of the EV charger can be rented for an additional fee.

Charging from ordinary sockets is a fire hazard and is not permitted.

🐑 Electric sheep fence

An electric sheep fence surrounds parts of the property.

Please close all gates after passing through so sheep do not enter the plot.

The fence has low voltage and is not dangerous, but small children should be supervised.

🔒 Private areas

Two rooms in the cabin and one cupboard in the TV unit are private and therefore locked.

Please respect these areas.

🛠 Damage and accidents

Accidents can happen.

If something is damaged, stops working or is missing, we would appreciate you letting us know as soon as possible.

That helps us keep the cabin in good condition for future guests as well.

❤️ Thank you

We hope you have a wonderful stay at Gravassryggen and take home many good memories from the mountains.

Thank you for helping us take care of the cabin.

📍 Address

Gravassryggen 18
4443 Tjørhom, Sirdal

Apple Maps and Google Maps will take you directly to the cabin.

🕒 Check-in

Check-in is from 16:00.

Early check-in may be possible for an additional fee if the cabin is ready. Please contact us well in advance, and we will see what is possible.

🛏 Bed linen, towels and cloths

Bed linen, towels, kitchen towels and dishcloths are not included in the stay.

Please bring:

  • Bed linen: sheet, duvet cover and pillowcase
  • Bath towel and hand towel
  • Kitchen towels and dishcloths

Rental of bed linen and bath towel:
See the next section.

Bed sizes

  • 1 bed – 180 × 200 cm
  • 1 bed – 150 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 120 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 80 × 200 cm

Pillow size: 50 × 70 cm
Duvet size: 140 × 200 cm

🛏 Bed linen rental

If you do not want to bring your own bed linen, this can be rented before arrival.

Price: 250 NOK per guest.

Each set includes:

  • 1 sheet
  • 1 duvet cover
  • 1 pillowcase
  • 1 bath towel

Rental must be ordered before arrival so that bed linen and towels can be prepared at the cabin.

🛒 Grocery store

The nearest grocery store is Joker Sinnes, about 5 minutes by car from the cabin.

The store offers groceries, fruit and vegetables, fresh bread, household items, fuel, EV charging and a small selection of pharmacy products.

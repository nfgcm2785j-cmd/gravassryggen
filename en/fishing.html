🤝 Exclusive fishing agreement in Gravvatn

We have a local agreement with the landowner that allows guests at Gravassryggen to fish in the agreed area.

The agreement applies only to guests staying at the cabin, and only within the area marked on the map below.

Please be considerate, follow local rules and take all waste back with you.

🗺 Map of the fishing area

The map below shows the area covered by the agreement. Our guests have exclusive fishing rights where the red property line meets the lake. The blue star on the map marks the cabin. Click the map to open a larger version.

🎫 Fishing permits

Most fishing lakes in Sirdal require a valid fishing permit. Everyone over 16 must buy a fishing permit before fishing.

Physical fishing permits can also be purchased at Sirdal Tourist Information at Kvæven.

🌿 Important to remember

  • Only fish in areas where you have a valid right or fishing permit.
  • Take all rubbish back with you.
  • Show consideration for landowners, animals and others using the area.
  • Follow local fishing rules and any restrictions.

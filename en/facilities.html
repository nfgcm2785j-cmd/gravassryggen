🏡 About the cabin

The cabin is 110 m² and enjoys an elevated, private location with panoramic views of the mountains and lake.

It has a bright, open-plan living room and kitchen with large windows, four bedrooms, one bathroom, one separate toilet, and a storage room by the entrance with space for shoes and outdoor equipment.

Outside is a terrace of more than 100 m², where you can enjoy sunshine from morning until evening and views of the mountains.

  • 🛏️ Four bedrooms
  • 🚿 One bathroom
  • 🚽 One separate toilet
  • 🍽️ Open-plan living room and kitchen
  • 📦 Storage room by the entrance
  • 🌞 Terrace of more than 100 m²

🌞 Outdoor areas

The cabin has a large, south-facing terrace of more than 100 m² with panoramic views of the mountains and lake. The terrace is for guests' exclusive use during their stay.

On the terrace you will find:

  • 🔥 Fire pit
  • 🥩 Charcoal barbecue
  • 🍽️ Dining table with benches
  • 🪑 Two comfortable wooden lounge chairs with a table

Please bring your own charcoal for the barbecue and firewood for the fire pit.

📶 Internet and entertainment

The cabin has Starlink internet with coverage throughout.

Typical speeds are around 100 Mbit/s download and 30 Mbit/s upload, but may vary depending on network load and weather.

The connection works well for streaming, video meetings, remote work and general use.

Network: Solsiden
The password is sent through Airbnb.

There is also 4G and 5G coverage in the area.

The living room has a 55-inch Samsung Smart TV with common streaming services available when you sign in with your own account. Sonos speakers are available in the kitchen and bathroom.

☕ Kitchen and dining area

The kitchen is fully equipped for everyday cooking and includes:

  • ☕ Moccamaster filter coffee maker
  • 🍳 Induction hob and oven
  • 🍽️ Dishwasher
  • 🧊 Fridge-freezer
  • 🥂 Wine and beer glasses
  • 🍴 Standard kitchen equipment

Please bring coffee and coffee filters if you would like to use the coffee maker.

The dining table can be extended with an additional leaf when more seating is needed.

🧺 Laundry, drying and practical equipment

The cabin has a washing machine, drying rack and two boot dryers available for use when needed.

💧 Drinking water

The cabin has fresh mountain well water.

The water is of very good quality and is safe to drink directly from the tap.

🔌 EV charging

The cabin has an Easee EV charger with a Mode 3 Type 2 connection.

Charging costs 3 NOK per kWh, based on actual consumption measured by the charger. It charges at up to 16 A and automatically adjusts the current according to available capacity.

EV charging must be agreed in advance.

Important: Electric vehicles must only be charged using the cabin's approved EV charger. Charging from ordinary power sockets is not permitted.

📖 Using the cabin

Important information about the toilet, water, heating, waste disposal and other practical matters during your stay.

← Back to the front page

🚽 Toilet

⚠️ Only toilet paper may be flushed down the toilet.

Wet wipes, paper towels, cotton buds, sanitary products, tampons, pads, food waste and all other waste must be placed in the rubbish bin.

Incorrect use may cause blocked pipes and costly damage, and may affect both your stay and future guests.

🗑 Waste disposal

There is no regular waste collection for cabins in Sirdal.

All waste must therefore be taken from the cabin and delivered to a local waste disposal point during your stay or before departure.

The nearest waste disposal point is by the entrance to Sirdal Skisenter, and most guests pass it when going shopping or heading out for activities.

📷 Security cameras

There are three outdoor security cameras installed on the property.

All cameras are turned off during guests' stays.

🚿 Water usage

The cabin area is supplied by a shared mountain well with clean drinking water.

Please use water with care. Avoid unnecessary water use and keep showers to a reasonable length so that enough water remains available throughout your stay.

Very high water usage may temporarily interrupt the supply while the well refills. This normally resolves itself after a while.

If the water supply stops, please let us know immediately. This allows us to check whether there is a fault that needs attention.

🔥 Heating and fireplace

The cabin is heated with panel heaters and underfloor heating. This is normally sufficient to maintain a comfortable temperature during spring, summer and autumn.

If you would like to use the fireplace, please use dry firewood only. The firewood stored outside in the shelter and by the entrance door is wet and cannot currently be used.

We recommend that the fireplace is only used by people experienced with wood-burning fireplaces.

🔒 Private rooms and installations

Some rooms, storage areas and installations are private and are not included in the rental.

  • 🚪 Two rooms are locked and used for private storage.
  • 👕 The wardrobe in the guest room is private.
  • ☕ The Gaggenau coffee machine is disconnected and cannot be used. A Moccamaster filter coffee maker is available.
  • 🎵 The music system in the living room is disconnected and cannot be used. Sonos speakers in the kitchen and bathroom are available.
  • ♨️ The hot tub is not available to guests.

🏡 About the cabin

The cabin is 110 m² and is located high and privately, with panoramic views of the mountains and the lake.

It has a bright, open-plan living room and kitchen with large windows, four bedrooms, one bathroom, one separate toilet, and a storage room by the entrance with space for shoes and outdoor equipment.

Outside the cabin there is a large terrace of more than 100 m², where you can enjoy the sun from morning to evening and the mountain views.

  • 🛏️ Four bedrooms
  • 🚿 One bathroom
  • 🚽 One separate toilet
  • 🍽️ Open-plan living room and kitchen
  • 📦 Storage room by the entrance
  • 🌞 Terrace of more than 100 m²

🌞 Outdoor areas

The cabin has a large, south-facing terrace of more than 100 m² with panoramic views of the mountains and the lake. The terrace is for the exclusive use of guests during the stay.

On the terrace you will find:

  • 🔥 Fire pit
  • 🥩 Charcoal grill
  • 🍽️ Dining table with benches
  • 🪑 Two comfortable wooden lounge chairs with a small table

Please bring your own charcoal for the grill and firewood for the fire pit.

📶 Internet and entertainment

The cabin has Starlink internet with coverage throughout the cabin.

Typical speed is around 100 Mbit/s download and 30 Mbit/s upload, but the speed may vary depending on network load and weather conditions.

The internet works well for streaming, video meetings, remote work and general use.

🧺 Laundry, drying and practical equipment

The cabin has a washing machine, drying rack and two boot dryers that can be used when needed.

🗑 Waste disposal

There is no regular waste collection for cabins in Sirdal.

All waste must therefore be taken from the cabin and delivered to a local waste disposal point during the stay or before departure.

The nearest waste disposal point is by the entrance to Sirdal Skisenter, and most guests pass it when going shopping or heading out for activities.

🛏 Bed linen and towels

Bed linen and towels can be rented for 250 NOK per set.

This must be ordered well before arrival so that it can be prepared at the cabin.

💧 Drinking water

The cabin has fresh mountain well water.

The water is of very good quality and is safe to drink directly from the tap.

🚿 Water usage

The cabin area is supplied by a shared mountain well with clean drinking water.

Please use water with care. Avoid unnecessary water use and keep showers to a reasonable length so that there is enough water available throughout the stay.

In the event of very high water usage, the water supply may temporarily stop while the well refills. This normally resolves itself after a while.

If the water supply stops, please let us know immediately. This allows us to check whether there is a fault that needs to be fixed.

🚽 Toilet

⚠️ Only toilet paper may be flushed down the toilet. ⚠️

Wet wipes, paper towels, cotton buds, sanitary products, tampons, pads, food waste and all other waste must be placed in the rubbish bin.

Incorrect use may cause blocked pipes and costly damage, and may affect both your stay and future guests.

🔥 Heating and fireplace

The cabin is heated with panel heaters and underfloor heating. This is normally sufficient to keep a comfortable temperature during spring, summer and autumn.

If you would like to use the fireplace, please use dry firewood only. The firewood stored outside in the shelter and by the entrance door is wet and cannot be used yet.

We recommend that the fireplace is only used by people who are used to lighting and managing a wood-burning fireplace.

If you visit the cabin in winter, you will find more information about the fireplace and heating on the page below.

🔌 EV charging

The cabin has an Easee EV charger with a Mode 3 Type 2 connection.

The price for charging is 3 NOK per kWh, based on actual consumption measured by the charger. The charger can charge at up to 16 A, but automatically adjusts the charging current according to available capacity.

EV charging must be agreed in advance.

Important: Electric vehicles must only be charged from the cabin's approved EV charger. Charging from ordinary power sockets is not allowed.

📷 Security and cameras

There are three outdoor security cameras installed on the property.

All cameras are turned off during guests' stays.

🦊 Wildlife

The area around the cabin has rich wildlife.

If you are lucky, you may see animals such as moose, red fox, hare, black grouse, badger, stoat or golden eagle.

Enjoy nature, respect the wildlife and keep a safe distance if you encounter wild animals.

ℹ️ Private facilities

Some rooms and installations at the cabin are private and are not included in the rental.

  • 🚪 Two rooms are closed off and used for private storage.
  • 👕 The wardrobe in the guest room is private.
  • ☕ The Gaggenau coffee machine is disconnected and cannot be used. A Moccamaster filter coffee maker is available.
  • 🎵 The music system in the living room is disconnected and cannot be used. Sonos speakers in the kitchen and bathroom are available for guests.
  • ♨️ The jacuzzi is not available for guests.

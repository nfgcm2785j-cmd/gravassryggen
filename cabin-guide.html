📩 Welcome

Welcome to Gravassryggen. We hope you have a relaxing and memorable stay in our family mountain cabin.

This guide contains practical information to help you during your stay.

🏡 Private Use of the Cabin

Your booking includes exclusive private use of the entire cabin and property.

The owner does not live on the property and will not visit during your stay unless requested by guests or in the event of an emergency.

🔑 Key

The key is located in the key box to the right of the entrance.

Please remember to lock the cabin whenever you leave. Before departure, return the key to the key box and lock it.

📶 WiFi & Mobile Coverage

High-speed Starlink internet is available throughout the cabin.

There is also 4G mobile coverage in the area.

💧 Drinking Water

The cabin is supplied with fresh mountain well water.

The water is safe to drink and of excellent quality.

The cabin area shares a common mountain well. During periods of high demand, excessive water consumption may temporarily reduce the water supply.

Please keep showers to a reasonable length and avoid unnecessary water consumption.

🚽 Toilets

⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT

To avoid blocked pipes, please flush toilet paper only.

Wet wipes, cotton swabs, paper towels, sanitary products, tampons and all other waste must be disposed of in the trash.

🔥 Heating

During spring, summer and autumn, the cabin is comfortably heated with electric panel heaters and underfloor heating.

As the cabin remains warm during these seasons, the fireplace is not available for guest use.

⚡ Electric Fence

An electric sheep fence surrounds parts of the property.

Please close all gates after passing through them to help keep sheep out of the area.

The fence carries a low-current electric pulse and is not dangerous, but small children should be supervised around it.

🏠 House Rules

To help preserve the cabin and ensure a pleasant experience for everyone, please observe the following rules:

  • Please remove outdoor shoes when entering the cabin.
  • No parties or events.
  • No smoking indoors.
  • Please respect the neighbours and keep noise to a reasonable level, especially between 11:00 PM and 7:00 AM.
  • Any damage or missing items should be reported as soon as possible.

Thank you for helping us take care of the cabin.

🔒 Private Areas

Two rooms in the cabin and one compartment in the TV cabinet are reserved for private storage and are therefore locked.

The jacuzzi, EV charger and built-in Gaggenau coffee machine are reserved for private use and are not included in the rental.

📷 Security Cameras

There are three outdoor security cameras installed at the property.

All cameras are switched off during guest stays.

🗑 Waste Disposal

Please note that there is no household waste collection service for cabins in Sirdal.

Guests must therefore dispose of their rubbish at one of the local waste collection stations during their stay or before departure.

Nearest waste collection station

Tjørhomfjellet Ski Centre – approximately 5 minutes by car
Address: Tjørhomfjellet 51
GPS: 58.90973 N, 6.84141 E

Alternative waste collection station

Svartvatn – approximately 8 minutes by car
Address: Sirdalsveien 7040

At the roundabout by Joker Sinnes, take the exit towards Tonstad. The waste collection station is located at the gravel area at the end of the lake.

GPS: 58.90132 N, 6.85481 E

If travelling towards Stavanger

Additional waste collection stations are available at several roadside parking areas in the Hunnedalen cabin area.

Search for: Høgaleitet Parking in Apple Maps
GPS: 58.87162 N, 6.58223 E

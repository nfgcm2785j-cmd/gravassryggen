📩 Welcome

Thank you for choosing Gravassryggen. We look forward to welcoming you to our family mountain cabin in Sirdal.

📍 Address

Gravassryggen 18
4443 Tjørhom
Sirdal, Norway

Apple Maps and Google Maps will take you directly to the cabin.

🕒 Check-in

Check-in is from 3:00 PM.

Early check-in may be available upon request for an additional fee, subject to the cleaning schedule and availability.

🚗 Parking

Parking is available directly at the cabin.

Additional parking is available in the shared parking area by the road, approximately a 3-minute walk from the cabin.

🛏 Bed Linen & Towels

Bed linen and towels are not included and must be brought by guests.

If you prefer, rental linen and towels can be arranged for NOK 250 per person. Please let me know well in advance.

Bed sizes

  • 1 bed – 180 × 200 cm
  • 1 bed – 150 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 120 × 200 cm
  • 2 beds – 80 × 200 cm

Pillow size: 50 × 70 cm
Duvet size: 140 × 200 cm

🛒 Grocery Store

The nearest grocery store is Joker Sinnes, approximately 5 minutes by car from the cabin.

The store offers groceries, fresh fruit and vegetables, fresh bread, household supplies, fuel, EV charging and basic pharmacy items.

🎣 Fishing

Sirdal offers excellent fishing opportunities in lakes and mountain waters.

Fishing permits for most waters can be purchased through Inatur.

Guests at Gravassryggen may also fish in selected parts of Gravann through an agreement with the local landowner.

Please contact me if you would like more information about where fishing is permitted.

🥾 Hiking & Activities

The cabin is located close to hiking trails, fishing waters and mountain areas.

More local recommendations will be added here soon.

🦊 Wildlife

The area is home to a variety of wildlife. If you are lucky, you may spot moose, red foxes, mountain hares, grouse, badgers, stoats or even golden eagles.

Wildlife sightings are never guaranteed, but many guests enjoy keeping an eye out while hiking or relaxing at the cabin.
